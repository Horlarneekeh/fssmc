ABOUT

Our mosque is dedicated to serving the community through worship, education, and support. We strive to promote Islamic knowledge, unity, and spiritual growth while providing a welcoming space for individuals and families to connect, learn, and grow in faith.

From weekly gatherings like Jumu’ah and educational sessions to special programs during Ramadan and Eid, there are opportunities for everyone to participate. We encourage all community members to join us, stay informed, and take part in these events as we grow in faith, knowledge, and unity together.

SERVICES

Our mosque is committed to nurturing faith, strengthening community bonds, and supporting the growth of future generations. Through our programs and initiatives, we aim to provide meaningful opportunities for learning, service, and engagement for individuals and families within the community.

ISLAMIC EDUCATION

Education is at the heart of a strong and thriving Muslim community. Our Islamic education programs are designed to provide authentic knowledge and spiritual guidance for people of all ages. Through classes, study circles, and lectures, participants can deepen their understanding of the Qur’an, Islamic teachings, and the values that shape a balanced and purposeful life. These programs aim to inspire continuous learning, strengthen faith, and help individuals apply Islamic principles in their daily lives.

COMMUNITY SERVICES

Serving the community is an essential part of our mission. We strive to create a supportive and compassionate environment where members of the community can come together to assist those in need. Through charitable initiatives, outreach programs, and volunteer opportunities, we encourage acts of kindness, generosity, and cooperation. Our community service efforts reflect the Islamic values of compassion, responsibility, and care for others.

YOUTH PROGRAMMMES

Our youth programs are designed to nurture the next generation by providing a positive and engaging environment for young Muslims. Through educational activities, mentorship, and community events, young people are encouraged to develop strong character, leadership skills, and a deeper connection to their faith. These programs aim to support personal growth, build confidence, and create meaningful connections among youth within the community.

VISION, MISSION AND CORE VALUES

VISION

Is to be a leading Masjid that builds a strong faith that is based on Allah-consciousness, piety and mindfulness of Allah, serving as spiritual shield against sins/wrongdoings.

MISSION

Is to provide deep knowledge of the Quran and Sunnah of the Prophet(PBOH) through teaching and learning, provide a safe, holy environment for worship while highlighting the true values of Islam through regular, modern and sustainable quality services for the care of the affairs of Muslims in our community and beyond.

CORE VALUES

To live a life of Quran and Sunnah as practised by the Prophet (PBOH) and exemplified by the Sahaba.
