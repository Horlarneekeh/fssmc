RAMADAN PROGRAMMES

DAILY TARAWEEH PRAYERS

Congregational Taraweeh prayers are held every night after the Isha prayer. These prayers offer an opportunity for the community to gather and listen to the recitation of the Qur’an while seeking spiritual growth and closeness to Allah.

QUR’AN RECITATION AND STUDY

Special sessions are organized to encourage the recitation, reflection, and understanding of the Qur’an. Participants are invited to join Qur’an circles and discussions aimed at strengthening their connection with the words of Allah.

ISLAMIC LECTURES AND REMINDERS

Throughout Ramadan, scholars and guest speakers deliver lectures and reminders that focus on faith, character, and the spiritual significance of the month.

CHARITY AND COMMUNITY SERVICE

Ramadan is a time of generosity and compassion. The mosque organizes charitable initiatives and donation drives to support those in need and to encourage acts of kindness within the community.

FSSS COMMUNITY CENTRAL MOSQUE

Our mosque is dedicated to serving the community through worship, education, and support. We strive to promote Islamic knowledge, unity, and spiritual growth while providing a welcoming space for individuals and families to connect, learn, and grow in faith.
