SPONSOR PROGRAMMES

Our Sponsor Programs provide members of the community with meaningful opportunities to support important initiatives that benefit individuals and families in need. Through these programs, sponsors can contribute to projects that promote education, welfare, and community development while earning the reward of ongoing charity. Through the generosity of sponsors, the mosque is able to expand its services and strengthen its role as a center of support, learning, and unity for the community. We invite community members to participate in these programs and contribute toward initiatives that create lasting positive impact. Together, through sincere intention and generosity, we can help support beneficial causes and uplift those in need.

FSSS COMMUNITY CENTRAL MOSQUE

Our mosque is dedicated to serving the community through worship, education, and support. We strive to promote Islamic knowledge, unity, and spiritual growth while providing a welcoming space for individuals and families to connect, learn, and grow in faith.
