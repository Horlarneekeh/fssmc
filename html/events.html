Ramadan Programmes

Daily Taraweeh Prayers

Congregational Taraweeh prayers are held every night after the Isha prayer. These offer an opportunity to listen to the Qur’an while seeking spiritual growth and closeness to Allah.

Qur’an Recitation & Study

Special sessions organized to encourage recitation, reflection, and understanding of the Qur’an. Participants are invited to join Qur’an circles and discussions.

Islamic Lectures & Reminders

Throughout Ramadan, scholars and guest speakers deliver lectures that focus on faith, character, and the spiritual significance of the holy month.

Charity & Community Service

Ramadan is a time of generosity. The mosque organizes charitable initiatives, iftar distributions, and donation drives to support those in need.
