ABOUT

OUR MOSQUE

Our mosque is a place of worship, learning, and community, dedicated to serving the spiritual and social needs of Muslims. It provides a welcoming environment where individuals and families can come together to pray, seek knowledge, and strengthen their connection with Allah. Beyond daily prayers, the mosque serves as a center for education, guidance, and community support. Through programs such as Qur’an classes, lectures, and outreach initiatives, we strive to nurture faith, promote good character, and support the well-being of our community. We are committed to fostering unity, compassion, and a strong sense of belonging. Our mosque welcomes everyone and aims to be a place where faith is strengthened, knowledge is shared, and meaningful connections are built.

HISTORY AND HERITAGE

Our mosque has long served as a center of worship, learning, and community, built on a strong foundation of faith, unity, and service. Since its establishment, it has been a place where individuals and families come together to pray, seek knowledge, and build meaningful connections within a supportive environment. Over the years, the mosque has grown alongside the community, preserving Islamic values while adapting to the evolving needs of its members. Through prayers, educational programs, and outreach initiatives, it continues to nurture faith, promote learning, and strengthen community bonds. Guided by the teachings of the Qur’an and the example of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, the mosque remains committed to upholding its rich heritage while serving as a source of guidance, unity, and inspiration for present and future generations.

FACILITIES AND AMENITIES

Our mosque offers a range of facilities to ensure a comfortable, welcoming, and accessible environment for all members of the community. These include spacious and well-maintained prayer areas for men and women, as well as clean ablution (wudu) facilities to support proper preparation for worship. The mosque also features classrooms and learning spaces for Qur’an classes and educational programs, along with a library that provides access to valuable Islamic resources. In addition, convenient parking is available for congregants, and accessibility features are in place to accommodate individuals with special needs. A multipurpose hall is also provided for community events, lectures, and gatherings, supporting the mosque’s role as a center for worship, learning, and community connection.

VISION, MISSION AND CORE VALUES

VISION

Is to be a leading Masjid that builds a strong faith that is based on Allah-consciousness, piety and mindfulness of Allah, serving as spiritual shield against sins/wrongdoings.

MISSION

Is to provide deep knowledge of the Quran and Sunnah of the Prophet(PBOH) through teaching and learning, provide a safe, holy environment for worship while highlighting the true values of Islam through regular, modern and sustainable quality services for the care of the affairs of Muslims in our community and beyond.

CORE VALUES

To live a life of Quran and Sunnah as practised by the Prophet (PBOH) and exemplified by the Sahaba.

LEADERSHIP AND IMAM

Our mosque is guided by a dedicated leadership team and Imam who oversee its activities and provide spiritual guidance. The Imam leads prayers, delivers sermons, and offers counseling, while the leadership ensures smooth operations and community support. Together, they foster faith, learning, and unity within the mosque.
