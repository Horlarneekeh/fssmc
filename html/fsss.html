About Us

Our mosque is dedicated to serving the community through worship, education, and support.

We strive to promote Islamic knowledge, unity, and spiritual growth while providing a welcoming space for individuals and families to connect, learn, and grow in faith.

Our Services

Committed to nurturing faith and strengthening community bonds.

Islamic Education

Providing authentic knowledge and spiritual guidance for people of all ages through classes and lectures.

Community Services

Creating a compassionate environment through charitable initiatives, outreach programs, and volunteer opportunities.

Youth Programs

Nurturing the next generation with educational activities, mentorship, and community events.

Vision

To be a leading Masjid that builds a strong faith based on Allah-consciousness, piety and mindfulness, serving as a spiritual shield.

Mission

Provide deep knowledge of the Quran and Sunnah, and a safe environment for worship through sustainable quality services.

Core Values

To live a life of Quran and Sunnah as practiced by the Prophet (PBUH) and exemplified by the Sahaba.
